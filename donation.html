How Lightning works

  1. Open a Lightning wallet (Wallet of Satoshi, Phoenix, Muun, etc.).
  2. Scan the QR code above with your wallet's camera or scanner.
  3. Confirm the amount in sats — any amount helps; ~150k sats ≈ one book.
  4. Payment settles in seconds; we allocate sats to the next book shipment batch.
