## Our story
Building a Bitcoin-native community across the Balkans
                    DuaCrypto started in Tirana in 2020 to bring people together around Bitcoin, self-custody, and Web3 education.
                    We help beginners and builders learn safely — in Albanian and English — without the noise of hype cycles.
                    Today we run meetups, publish guides, and support companies exploring Bitcoin in Albania.
                    Join 10,000+ members learning and collaborating every day.
                    Community education in Albanian & English
                    Meetups and Web3 guidance across the Balkans
                    Self-custody and corporate Bitcoin pathways
                    Crypto-native only — Bitcoin & Lightning, no Stripe or card checkout
