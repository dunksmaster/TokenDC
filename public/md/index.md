## FAQs

                
Frequently Asked Questions

            

            
                
                    How does Dua Crypto assist international businesses navigating crypto and Bitcoin regulations in Albania?
                    We provide specialized corporate consulting on Albania's Law No. 66/2020 "On Financial Markets Based on Distributed Ledger Technology." We guide enterprise clients through Financial Supervisory Authority (AMF) compliance, National Agency for Information Society (AKSHI) provisions, anti-money laundering (AML/KYC) obligations, and corporate tax structuring for crypto operations within the jurisdiction.
                
                
                    What are the legal requirements for establishing a cryptocurrency or Bitcoin mining operation in Albania?
                    Crypto mining businesses must comply with local commercial licensing, environmental regulations, and specific energy sector frameworks governed by the Energy Regulatory Authority (ERE). Our consulting services assist corporate entities in analyzing grid capacity, securing legal power supply agreements, and establishing compliant infrastructure footprints.
                
                
                    How do Peer-to-Peer (P2P) exchanges operate under current Albanian financial regulations?
                    Entities facilitating digital asset exchange or P2P matching platforms must align with local Virtual Asset Service Provider (VASP) regulations. We consult businesses on implementing required transaction monitoring, fiat-to-crypto escrow frameworks, and compliance reporting required by Albanian financial intelligence units to mitigate fraud and regulatory risk.
                
                
                    I am an absolute beginner with zero technical skills. Can I learn about Bitcoin, P2P, and mining safely here?
                    Yes. Dua Crypto serves as an educational hub designed for non-technical individuals. We break down how Bitcoin works, how to safely navigate P2P transactions without falling victim to scams, and the basic concepts of mining using clear, plain language in both Albanian and English without technical jargon.
                
                
                    Does Dua Crypto provide corporate advisory for institutional liquidity and OTC setups in Albania?
                    Yes. We offer strategic B2B consulting for enterprises requiring large-scale Over-the-Counter (OTC) routing, corporate treasury allocation into Bitcoin, and institutional liquidity management. All advisory pathways are tailored to maintain strict alignment with local banking policies and regional compliance standards.
