DAL: Bitcoin for Corporations Explained | DuaCrypto Blog
The Digital Asset Leaders Association (DAL) is DuaCrypto's enterprise program for companies exploring Bitcoin treasury strategy, regulatory alignment, and executive networking in the Balkans.
## Who it's for
CTOs, directors, corporate treasurers, and founders building Bitcoin-first organizations who need peers, practical resources, and local regulatory context — not hype.
## What you get
$99/year Lightning membership unlocks a private Telegram group, treasury working sessions, and consulting on Durana Tech Park residency for qualifying crypto and tech companies in Albania.
## How to join
Visit the [Bitcoin for Corporations](../bitcoin-for-corporations.html) page, pay via Lightning, and email your receipt to info@duacrypto.com for verification within one business day.
