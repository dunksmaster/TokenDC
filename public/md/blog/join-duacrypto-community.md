How to Join the DuaCrypto Community | DuaCrypto Blog
DuaCrypto is Albania's longest-running Bitcoin and crypto community. Joining is free — here's how to get started.
## 1. Join Telegram
Our primary hub is [t.me/dua_crypto](https://t.me/dua_crypto) with 10,000+ members. Introduce yourself, ask questions, and follow event announcements.
## 2. Read free guides
Long-form education lives at [duacrypto.org](https://duacrypto.org/) — Bitcoin basics, wallet setup, and Balkans-specific context.
## 3. Attend a meetup
We host monthly events in Tirana — Bitcoin Pizza Day, Balkans Crypto side sessions, and beginner workshops. Check our [events page](../events.html).
## 4. Go deeper
Corporate teams: explore [DAL membership](../bitcoin-for-corporations.html). Researchers: subscribe to our [premium newsletter](../newsletter.html).
