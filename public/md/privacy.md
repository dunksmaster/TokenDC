Privacy Policy | DuaCrypto
Last updated: May 29, 2025
## 1. Who we are
DuaCrypto ("we", "us") operates duacrypto.com and related community channels. We are Albania's first crypto community, based in Tirana. Contact: [info@duacrypto.com](mailto:info@duacrypto.com).
## 2. Information we collect
                Contact forms & newsletter: email address and any message you submit via Formspree or similar forms.
- Community platforms: username and messages you post on Telegram, X, or other public channels we operate.
- Analytics: anonymized usage data via Google Analytics (page views, device type, approximate location).
- Donations: blockchain transaction hashes and wallet addresses when you donate crypto — we do not collect traditional payment card data on this site.
## 3. How we use your information
We use collected data to respond to inquiries, send community updates you opt into, improve our Bitcoin and Web3 education content, and operate events in Tirana and across the Balkans. We do not sell personal data to third parties.
## 4. Cookies & analytics
We use cookies and similar technologies for site functionality and Google Analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings. See Google's privacy policy for analytics data handling.
## 5. Third-party services
We use third-party services including Google Analytics, Formspree, Telegram, and social media embeds. Each has its own privacy policy. Links to external sites (exchanges, partners) are not controlled by DuaCrypto.
## 6. Data retention & security
We retain contact and newsletter data while you remain subscribed or as needed for legitimate business purposes. We apply reasonable technical measures to protect data but cannot guarantee absolute security of information transmitted online.
## 7. Your rights
You may request access, correction, or deletion of personal data we hold by emailing [info@duacrypto.com](mailto:info@duacrypto.com). You may unsubscribe from newsletters at any time via the link in our emails or by contacting us.
## 8. Changes
We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our site after changes constitutes acceptance.
See also our [Terms of Service](terms.html).
