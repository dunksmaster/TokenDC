Our story

Building a Bitcoin-native community across the Balkans

DuaCrypto started in Tirana in 2020 to bring people together around Bitcoin, self-custody, and Web3 education. We help beginners and builders learn safely — in Albanian and English — without the noise of hype cycles.

Today we run meetups, publish guides, and support companies exploring Bitcoin in Albania. Join 10,000+ members learning and collaborating every day.
