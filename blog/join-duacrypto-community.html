How to Join the DuaCrypto Community

DuaCrypto is Albania’s longest-running Bitcoin and crypto community. Joining is free — here’s how to get started.

1. Join Telegram

Our primary hub is t.me/dua_crypto with 10,000+ members. Introduce yourself, ask questions, and follow event announcements.

2. Read free guides

Long-form education lives at duacrypto.org — Bitcoin basics, wallet setup, and Balkans-specific context.

3. Attend a meetup

We host monthly events in Tirana — Bitcoin Pizza Day, Balkans Crypto side sessions, and beginner workshops. Check our events page.

4. Go deeper

Corporate teams: explore DAL membership. Researchers: subscribe to our premium newsletter.
